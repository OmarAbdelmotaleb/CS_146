I got to meet a friend I knew for years in New York for the first time.

Meeting an online friend.

I met this one friend in an online community while playing a game and we didn't really know each other too well or talk to each other much for a while. We obviously knew who we were but didn't hang out much.

Later though, a mutual friend had brought us all together, along with a bunch of other friends met in that online community, and we ended up talking ever since. We found we had a lot in common. He's currently a senior in high school and he lived in New York.

I didn't have the ability to go by myself really due to time constraints and other factors, like parents at the time. Luckily, I was able to tour New York and see a bunch of places during pre-orientation at Stevens. I convinced him to meet me at Central Park and was able to see him for the first time. We got to chill for a bit but eventually had to head out. It was great getting to see him for the first time and I look forward to being able to see a few of my other friends in New York in the future.
