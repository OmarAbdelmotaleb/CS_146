Omar's Education

Constable Elementary School

I didn't have a favorite class. They all sucked.

I remember the teacher making everyone have to sit silently in the lunchroom because of a few loud kids. Pretty sure one of the 5th graders slapped her.

Crossroads North Middle School

My favorite class was Geometry in 8th grade.

I remember having to use lockers and getting on the bus, only to realize I forgot a binder and had to dash all the way in to get it. I hate lockers.

South Brunswick High School

My favorite class was a Computer Science course, Data Structures and Gaming in 12th grade.

I remember making my first game in sophmore year for a club in Python. It was a bootleg brickbreaker.
